Cape Point Tour

Explore the Atlantic Route

Cape Point Tour R1,700/Per Person

Discover the Beauty of the Atlantic Coast

This full-day tour takes you along the stunning Atlantic coastline, featuring iconic stops like Boulders Beach, Cape Point, and the Cape of Good Hope. Enjoy scenic drives, wildlife encounters, and breathtaking views.

Tour Details:

  • Duration: Full Day (8 hours)
  • Timing: 8:45 AM - 5:00 PM
  • Includes: Entry fee at Cape Point, Chapman’s Peak
  • Excluded:
    • Boat to Seal Island: R60 to R150 (depending on age)
    • Penguin entry at Boulders Beach: R215 (international) / R100 (SADC)
    • Cape Point Funicular: R90
