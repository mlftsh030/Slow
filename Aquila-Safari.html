Aquila Safari

Experience the Big Five in the Wild

Aquila Safari R1500/Per Person

Discover the Big Five

This safari takes you to Aquila Private Game Reserve, where you can see the Big Five (lion, leopard, elephant, rhino, and buffalo) in their natural habitat. Enjoy game drives, wildlife sightings, and the beauty of the South African bushveld.

Tour Details:

  • Duration: Full Day (7:45 AM - 6:00 PM)
  • Destinations: Aquila Safari
  • Includes: Still water, snacks (platters), transport, and game drives.
  • Excluded:
    • Lunch
    • Personal expenses

    • Large Car: R4500 (5 pax)
